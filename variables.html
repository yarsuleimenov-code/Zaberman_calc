Physical Volume

Physical Volume = (Length × Width × Height × Quantity) / 1728

Effective Volume

Effective Volume = Physical Volume × Stackability Coefficient

Total Effective Volume

Total Effective Volume = SUM(Item Effective Volume)

Vehicle Fit

Vehicle Selected = First Active Vehicle Where Capacity ≥ Total Effective Volume AND Max Weight ≥ Total Weight

Vehicle Count

Vehicle Count = CEILING(Total Effective Volume / Vehicle Capacity)

Internal Fuel Price

Internal Fuel Price = Current Avg. × (1 + Fuel Surcharge % / 100)

Fuel Cost

Fuel Cost = Distance / Loaded MPG × Internal Fuel Price

Mileage Cost

Mileage Cost = Distance × Cost Per Mile

Pickup / Delivery Labor Cost

Labor Cost = MAX(Min Loading Time, Weight × Handling Time Per lb) × Wage Per Minute × Crew Size

Heavy Item Handling

Heavy Handling Fee = IF(Item Weight > Max One Person Weight, Heavy Handling Rate, 0)

Unacceptable Weight Flag

Manual Review = IF(Item Weight > Unacceptable Weight, TRUE, FALSE)

Packaging Cost

Packaging Cost = SUM(Packaging Price Per Item × Quantity)

Crate Surface Area

Crate Surface Area = 2 × (L × W + L × H + W × H) / 144

Crate Material Cost

Crate Material Cost = Crate Surface Area × Crate Material Cost Per sq ft

Crate Labor Cost

Crate Labor Cost = Crate Surface Area × Crate Labor Minutes Per sq ft × Wage Per Minute

Crate Cost

Crate Cost = MAX(Minimum Crate Cost, Crate Material Cost + Crate Labor Cost)

Storage Cost

Storage Cost = Total Effective Volume × Storage Rate Per cu ft Per Day × Storage Days

Stairs Fee

Stairs Fee = MAX(0, Floor - 3) × Stairs Rate Per Floor

Long Carry Fee

Long Carry Fee = Long Carry Distance × Long Carry Rate Per ft

NYC Zone Fees

NYC Fees = Toll Allowance + Parking Allowance + Ticket Risk Reserve

Full Coverage Fee

Full Coverage Fee = Declared Value × Full Coverage Rate

Basic Liability Coverage

Basic Liability Coverage = Total Weight × 0.60

Consolidation Discount

Consolidation Discount = Eligible Charge × Discount %

Exclusive Delivery Fee

Exclusive Delivery Fee = Operational Cost × Exclusive Delivery Multiplier

Operational Cost

Operational Cost = Pickup Cost + Interstate Cost + Delivery Cost

Additional Charges

Additional Charges = Packaging Cost + Crate Cost + Storage Cost + Insurance Fee + Access Fees + Zone Fees

Margin

Margin = (Operational Cost + Additional Charges) × Margin Rate

Raw Final Price

Raw Final Price = Operational Cost + Additional Charges + Margin - Discounts

Rounded Final Price

Rounded Final Price = CEILING(Raw Final Price, Rounding Rule)

Цель страницы

Variables.html используется как административный слой pricing engine. Страница управляет коэффициентами, operational rules, vehicle logic, fuel pricing и formula governance.

Broker не взаимодействует с variables напрямую. Broker работает только с quote screen.

Архитектурный принцип

  • UI не должен содержать pricing logic.
  • Все расчёты должны выполняться backend pricing engine.
  • Variables являются input layer для pricing engine.
  • HTML screens отображают результат расчётов, а не вычисляют его.

Formula Governance

  • Каждое изменение variables должно создавать новую formula version.
  • Новые variables применяются только к новым estimates.
  • Старые estimates не должны пересчитываться задним числом.
  • Estimate должен хранить formula version и variables snapshot.

Operational Logic

  • Vehicle selection выполняется автоматически по Effective Volume и Weight.
  • Non-stackable items учитывают воздух над предметом.
  • Crating влияет одновременно на cost, handling и effective volume.
  • Fragile items должны увеличивать handling complexity.
  • NYC operational risks должны закладываться в zone fees.

Separation of Roles

  • Broker создаёт quote.
  • Admin управляет pricing variables.
  • Pricing engine рассчитывает стоимость.
  • Breakdown screen объясняет расчёт.

Future Scalability

  • Поддержка нескольких regional pricing models.
  • Поддержка dynamic fuel pricing.
  • Поддержка route-based pricing.
  • Поддержка automated dispatch estimation.
  • Поддержка ML/rule-based pricing adjustments в будущем.
